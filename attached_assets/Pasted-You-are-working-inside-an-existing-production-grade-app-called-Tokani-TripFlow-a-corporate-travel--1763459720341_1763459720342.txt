1. TARGET BUSINESS FLOW (WHAT WE WANT TO ACHIEVE)

The system should support this two-stage approval with RFQs and quotes:

Coordinator creates a travel request (New Request page).

Coordinator submits it for manager pre-approval to go and get quotes.

Manager approves pre-approval (or rejects/returns).

After pre-approval, coordinator sends RFQs (request for quotes) to preferred vendors via email from within TripFlow.

Vendors reply outside the system; coordinator logs each quote into TripFlow:

Vendor name, quote value, currency, PNR, expiry, attachment of quote, etc.

Once enough quotes are captured (as required by policy), coordinator submits the request for final approval with a selected quote and justification.

Manager (or finance) reviews quotes, compares options, and gives final approval for booking.

Once final approval is given, the Travel Desk processes/tickets the booking.

The number of quotes required should be driven by policy/config and checked before final approval.
